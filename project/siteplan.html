Home Care for Seniors - Site Plan

Site Name

Compassionate Senior Care - Providing quality home care services with love and dedication.

Site Purpose

To showcase the home care services offered, build trust with testimonials, share valuable caregiving tips, and provide easy contact options for potential clients.

Scenarios

  • What home care services are available for my elderly parents?
  • How can I book a consultation for senior care services?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#2C3E50) - Used for headings and navigation.
  • Light Gray (#ECF0F1) - Background color for sections.
  • Green (#27AE60) - Used for buttons and highlights.

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat
  • Body text: Arial
